Refund Policy

Sgananda Divine Beds Pty Ltd does not offer refunds on any of our products. All beds and mattresses are strictly non-refundable. In the unlikely event of a product issue, an inspection and repair will be conducted to resolve the matter efficiently and professionally. A bed or mattress may only be returned for an exchange if: - The product has not been unwrapped or used, and - The customer wishes to exchange it for a higher-quality or upgraded mattress. All exchange requests are subject to review and approval by our customer service team.

Shipping and Delivery Policy

We proudly offer same-day deliveries to ensure our customers receive their products promptly. Our delivery hours are 09:30 to 17:30, Monday to Saturday. Customers are kindly requested to ensure availability during these hours to receive their orders. Should there be any unforeseen delays, our logistics team will promptly communicate with the customer to arrange suitable delivery adjustments.
